About Vivo V20 Pro

Here a lot of leaks have come out about Vivo V20 Pro its specification amazes you. Vivo V20 Pro smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system Android 10, Funtouch 11. The phone is powered by an Octa-core (1x2.4 GHz Kryo 475 Prime & 1x2.2 GHz Kryo 475 Gold & 6x1.8 GHz Kryo 475 Silver) and it runs on Qualcomm SDM765 Snapdragon 765G (7 nm).

Vivo V20 Pro smartphone has an AMOLED display with 1080 x 2400 pixels screen resolution and 409 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 44-megapixel wide and 8-megapixel ultrawide primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 64 megapixel wide-angle, 8-megapixel ultrawide, and 2-megapixel depth. camera setup of Vivo V20 Pro include HDR, panorama, dual-LED dual-tone flash, and more. It has support for 33W fast charging and non-removable 4000 mAh battery.

Vivo V20 Pro price in India.

Vivo V20 Pro price in India start from Rs. 29,999 and Vivo V20 Pro expected release date is 02 December 2020. This smartphone comes with single variants like 8/128GB RAM. It is available at three color options like Sunset Melody, Midnight Jazz, and Moonlight Sonata.
