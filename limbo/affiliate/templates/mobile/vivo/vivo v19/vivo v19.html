Vivo V19 smartphone comes with the 6.44-inch full HD (1080*2400 pixels) Super AMOLED capacitive touch display. The Vivo V19 is powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 712 octa core chipset clocked at 2.3GHz paired with 128GB 8GB RAM and 256GB 8GB RAM and it also had dedicated micro-SD slot which gives option expand your memory through SD card. It has glass on both the sides front as well as back. But, the body of the smartphone is build with plastic frame which gives the phone a descent look. The Vivo V19 comes with the quad rear camera 48MP wide angle and standard camera, 8MP ultrawide angle camera, 2MP macro camera and 2MP depth sensing camera. It comes 32MP selfie camera which supports video recording at 1080@30fps. It comes 8MP selfie camera which supports video recording at 1080@30fps. The smartphone is powered by Non-removable 4500mah battery which support fast charging at 18W. It comes with fingerprint scanner at the rear of the phone which make easier to user to unlock the phone and it supports 4G LTE network.It runs on Android 9.0 (Pie), Funtouch OS 9.2.
