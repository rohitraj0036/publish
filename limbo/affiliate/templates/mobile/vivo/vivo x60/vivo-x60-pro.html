About Vivo X60 Pro

Here a lot of leaks have come out about Vivo X60 Pro its specification amazes you. Vivo X60 Pro smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system Android v10 (Q). The phone is powered by an Octa-core (2.8GHz, Single core, Kryo 475 + 2.4GHz, Kryo 475 + 1.8GHz, Hexa Core, Kryo 475) and it runs on Qualcomm Snapdragon 768G.

Vivo X60 Pro smartphone has an AMOLED display with 1080 x 2400 pixels screen resolution and 385 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 32-megapixel wide primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 64 megapixel wide-angle, 16-megapixel ultrawide-angle, 12-megapixel Telephoto Camera, and 8-megapixel micro camera setup of Vivo X60 Pro include Auto Flash, Auto Focus, Face detection, Touch to focus, HDR, panorama, and more. It has support for fast charging support and having 4700 mAh mAh non-removable battery.

Vivo X60 Pro price in India.

Vivo X60 Pro price in India start from Rs. 51,990 and Vivo X60 Pro expected release date is 29 December 2020. This smartphone comes with two variants like 128GB 12GB RAM, and 256GB 12GB RAM. It is available at three color options like grey, shimmer (translated), and a blueish-pink gradient.
