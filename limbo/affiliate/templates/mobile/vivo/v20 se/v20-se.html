About Vivo V20 SE

Here a lot of leaks have come out about Vivo V20 SE its specification amazes you. Vivo V20 SE smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system (Android 10). The phone is powered by an Octa-core (4x2.0 GHz Kryo 260 Gold & 4x1.8 GHz Kryo 260 Silver) processor and it runs on Qualcomm SDM665 Snapdragon 665 (11 nm).

Vivo V20 SE smartphone has an Super AMOLED display with 1080 x 2400 pixels screen resolution and 409 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 32-megapixel primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 48 megapixel wide-angle, 8 megapixels ultra-wide, and 2-megapixels depth. camera with features like Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus, and Continuos Shooting.
