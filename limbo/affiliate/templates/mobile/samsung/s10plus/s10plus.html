Samsung Galaxy S10+ smartphone was launched on 2019, February 20. Samsung Galaxy S10+ is powered by a Octa-core (2x2.73 GHz Mongoose M4 & 2x2.31 GHz Cortex-A75 & 4x1.95 GHz Cortex-A55) - EMEA/LATAM Samsung Exynos 9820 (8 nm) - EMEA/LATAM processor . It comes with 12GB and 8GB of RAM.The Samsung Galaxy S10+ on the rear packs a 12-megapixel primary camera, 12-megapixel (telephoto) and 16-megapixel ultrawide-angle-camera. The rear camera setup has autofocus. It sports a 10-megapixel camera on the front for selfies, with an f/1.9 aperture and 8-megapixel depth senson. The front camera also features autofocus.There's a 4100mAh battery, with support for fast charging.

The S10+ runs on OneUI 2, which is based on Android 10.The Samsung Galaxy S10+ measures 157.6 x 74.1 x 7.8 mm (6.20 x 2.92 x 0.31 in) (height x width x thickness) and weighs 175 g / 198 g (ceramic) (6.17 oz). It was launched in Prism White, Prism Black, Prism Green, Prism Blue, Canary Yellow, Flamingo Pink, Ceramic Black, Ceramic White, Cardinal Red, Smoke Blue .Connectivity options on the Samsung Galaxy S10+ include Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/6, dual-band, Wi-Fi Direct, hotspot, GPS, Bluetooth v5.00, NFC, USB Type-C, 3G, and 4G.
