Samsung Galaxy Note 10 smartphone was launched on 2019, August 07. Samsung Galaxy Note 10 is powered by a Octa-core (2x2.73 GHz Mongoose M4 & 2x2.4 GHz Cortex-A75 & 4x1.9 GHz Cortex-A55) - EMEA/LATAM Samsung Exynos 9825 (7 nm) - EMEA/LATAM processor . It comes with 8GB of RAM.The Samsung Galaxy Note 10 on the rear packs a 12-megapixel primary camera, 12-megapixel (telephoto) and 16-megapixel ultrawide-angle-camera . The rear camera setup has autofocus. It sports a 10-megapixel camera on the front for selfies, with an f/2.2 aperture . The front camera also features autofocus.There's a 3500mAh battery, with support for fast charging.

The Note 10 runs on One UI 2.1, which is based on Android 10.The Samsung Galaxy Note 10 measures 151 x 71.8 x 7.9 mm (5.94 x 2.83 x 0.31 in) (height x width x thickness) and weighs 168 g (5.93 oz). It was launched in Aura Glow, Aura White, Aura Black, Aura Pink, Aura Red. Connectivity options on the Samsung Galaxy Note 10 include Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/6, dual-band, Wi-Fi Direct, hotspot, GPS, Bluetooth v5.00, NFC, USB Type-C, 3G, and 4G.
