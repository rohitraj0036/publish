Samsung Galaxy S20 ultra smartphone was launched on 2020, February 11. Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra is powered by a Octa-core (2x2.73 GHz Mongoose M5 & 2x2.50 GHz Cortex-A76 & 4x2.0 GHz Cortex-A55) Samsung Exynos 990 (7 nm+) processor . It comes with 12GB of RAM.The Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra on the rear packs a 108-megapixel primary camera, a 48-megapixel telephoto camera, 12-megapixel ultrawide-angle-camera and a depth vision camera. The rear camera setup has autofocus. It sports a 40-megapixel camera on the front for selfies, with an f/2.2 aperture. The front camera also features autofocus.There's a 5,000mAh battery, with support for fast charging.

The S20 runs on OneUI 2, which is based on Android 10.The Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra measures 166.9 x 76 x 8.8 mm (6.57 x 2.99 x 0.35 in) (height x width x thickness) and weighs 220 g (7.76 oz). It was launched in Cosmic Grey, Cosmic Black, Cloud White.Connectivity options on the Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra include Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/6, dual-band, Wi-Fi Direct, hotspot, GPS, Bluetooth v5.00, NFC, USB Type-C, 3G, and 4G.
