About Samsung Galaxy A42

Here a lot of leaks have come out about Samsung Galaxy A42 its specification amazes you. Samsung Galaxy A42 smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system (Android 10). The phone is powered by an Octa-core (2.2 GHz, Dual core, Kryo 570 + 1.8 GHz, Hexa Core, Kryo 570) processor and it runs on Qualcomm Snapdragon 750G.
