Samsung Galaxy Note 10+ smartphone was launched on 2019, August 07. Samsung Galaxy Note 10+ is powered by a Octa-core (2x2.73 GHz Mongoose M4 & 2x2.4 GHz Cortex-A75 & 4x1.9 GHz Cortex-A55) - EMEA/LATAM Samsung Exynos 9825 (7 nm) - EMEA/LATAM processor . It comes with 12GB of RAM.The Samsung Galaxy Note 10+ on the rear packs a 12-megapixel primary camera, 12-megapixel (telephoto) and 16-megapixel ultrawide-angle-camera and 0.3-megapixel (depth sensor). The rear camera setup has autofocus. It sports a 10-megapixel camera on the front for selfies, with an f/2.2 aperture . The front camera also features autofocus.There's a 4300mAh battery, with support for fast charging.

The Note 10+ runs on OneUI 2.0, which is based on Android 10.The Samsung Galaxy Note 10+ measures 162.3 x 77.2 x 7.9 mm (6.39 x 3.04 x 0.31 in) (height x width x thickness) and weighs 196 g (6.91 oz). It was launched in Aura Glow, Aura White, Aura Black, Aura Blue. Connectivity options on the Samsung Galaxy Note 10+ include Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/6, dual-band, Wi-Fi Direct, hotspot, GPS, Bluetooth v5.00, NFC, USB Type-C, 3G, and 4G.
