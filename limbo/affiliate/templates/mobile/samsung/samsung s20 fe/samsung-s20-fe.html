About Samsung Galaxy S20 FE

Here a lot of leaks have come out about Samsung Galaxy S20 FE its specification amazes you. Samsung Galaxy S20 FE smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system (Android 10). The phone is powered by an Octa-core (1x2.84 GHz Kryo 585 & 3x2.42 GHz Kryo 585 & 4x1.80 GHz Kryo 585) processor and it runs on Qualcomm SM8250 Snapdragon 865 (7 nm+).

Samsung Galaxy S20 FE smartphone has an Super AMOLED display with 1080 x 2400 pixels screen resolution and 402 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 32-megapixel primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 12 megapixel wide-angle, 12 megapixels ultra-wide and 8-megapixels telephoto. camera with features like Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus. It has support for 15W fast charging and non-removable 4500 mAh battery.
