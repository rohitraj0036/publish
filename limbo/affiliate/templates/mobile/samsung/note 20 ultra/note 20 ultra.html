About Samsung Galaxy Note 20 ultra

Samsung has launched its new phone Samsung Galaxy Note 20 ultra in India on August 5. 2020. in term of display, Samsung Galaxy Note 20 ultra have 6.9 (in inch) with Dynamic AMOLED 2X Infinity-O touchscreen display. The phone is powered by a 2.73GHz octa-core Exynos 990 SoC processor. The phone runs Android 10 and is powered by a 4500mAh non-removable battery. The Samsung Galaxy Note 20 ultra has supports for the 5G network in some regions.

Samsung Galaxy Note 20 ultra smartphone has an Dynamic AMOLED 2X Infinity-O touchscreen, 16M colors. It has a resolution of 1440x3200 pixels and 496 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 10-megapixel primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 108-megapixel Wide-angle Camera, 12 megapixels ultra-wide, and 12MP Telephoto Camera. camera with features like auto flash, face detection, digital zoom, and touch to focus. It has support for fast charging and has a non-removable 4500mAh mAh battery.
