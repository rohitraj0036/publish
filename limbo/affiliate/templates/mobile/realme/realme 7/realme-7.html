About Realme 7

Realme is now going to launch a new smartphone, takes it's series one step further, its name is Realme 7. Realme 7 smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system (Android 10). The phone is powered by an Octa-core (2.05 GHz, Dual core, Cortex A76 + 2 GHz, Hexa Core, Cortex A55) processor and it runs on MediaTek Helio G90T Chipset.

Realme 7 smartphone has an IPS LCD display with 1080 x 2400 Pixels screen resolution and 395 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 16-megapixel primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 64 megapixel wide-angle, 8 megapixels ultra-wide, 2-megapixel micro and 2-megapixels depth. camera with features like Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus. It has support for 30W fast charging and non-removable 5000 mAh battery.

Realme 7 price in India.

Realme 7 price in India start from Rs 13,999 and Realme 7 release date is 2020 September 01. This smartphone comes with single variants like 6/64GB RAM. It is available at one color options like Mist Blue and Mist White.
