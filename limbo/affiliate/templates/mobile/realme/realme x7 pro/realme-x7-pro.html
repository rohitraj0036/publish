About Realme X7 Pro

Realme is going to launch Realme X7 Pro soon, taking X series one step further. Realme X7 Pro smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system (Android 10). The phone is powered by an Octa-core (4x2.6 GHz Cortex-A77 & 4x2.0 GHz Cortex-A55) processor and it runs on Mediatek Dimensity 1000+ (7nm) Chipset.

Realme X7 Pro smartphone has an Super AMOLED capacitive touchscreen with 1080 x 2400 Pixels screen resolution and 402 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 32-megapixel primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 64 megapixel wide-angle, 8 megapixels ultra-wide, 2-megapixels micro, and 2-megapixels depth. camera with features like Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus. It has support for 65W fast charging and non-removable 4500 mAh battery.

Realme X7 Pro price in India.

Realme X7 Pro price in India start from Rs. 23,490 and Realme X7 Pro release date is 2020 November 19 ( Expected Date ). This smartphone comes with two variants like 128GB 6GB RAM, 128GB 8GB RAM, and 256GB 8GB RAM. It is available at two color options like Dark Blue, White, and Rainbow.
