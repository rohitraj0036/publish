About Realme Narzo 20A

Realme is going to launch Realme Narzo 20A soon, taking its Narzo series one step further. Realme Narzo 20A smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system (Android 10). The phone is powered by an Octa-core (2.05 GHz, Dual core, Cortex A76 + 2 GHz, Hexa Core, Cortex A55) processor and it runs on MediaTek Helio G35 Chipset.
