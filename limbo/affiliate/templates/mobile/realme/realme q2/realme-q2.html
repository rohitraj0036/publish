About Realme Q2

Realme is going to launch Realme Q2 soon, taking Q2 series one step further. Realme Q2 smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system (Android 10, Realme UI). The phone is powered by an Octa-core (2x2.4 GHz Cortex-A76 & 6x2.0 GHz Cortex-A55) processor and it runs on MediaTek Dimensity 800U 5G (7 nm) Chipset.

Realme Q2 smartphone has IPS LCD capacitive touchscreen with 1080 x 2400 Pixels screen resolution, 120Hz screen refresh rate and 402 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 16-megapixel primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 48 megapixel wide-angle, 8 megapixels ultra-wide, and 2-megapixels micro. camera with features like Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus. It has support for 35W fast charging and non-removable 5000 mAh battery.

Realme Q2 price in India.

Realme Q2 price in India start from Rs 19,990 and Realme Q2 release date is 2020, October 19 ( Expected Date ). This smartphone comes with two variants like 128GB 4GB RAM and 128GB 6GB RAM. It is available at two color options like Blue and Violet.
