About Realme 7 Pro

Realme is now going to launch a new smartphone, takes it's series one step further, its name is Realme 7 Pro. Realme 7 Pro smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system (Android 10). The phone is powered by an Octa-core (2.4 GHz, Single core, Kryo 475 + 2.2 GHz, Single core, Kryo 475 + 1.8 GHz, Hexa Core, Kryo 475) processor and it runs on Qualcomm Snapdragon 765G Chipset.

Realme 7 Pro smartphone has an Super AMOLED display with 1080 x 2400 Pixels screen resolution and 395 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 32-megapixel primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 64 megapixel wide-angle, 8 megapixels ultra-wide, 2-megapixel micro and 2-megapixels depth. camera with features like Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus. It has support for 65W fast charging and non-removable 4500 mAh battery.

Realme 7 Pro price in India.

Realme 7 Pro price in India start from Rs 17,999 and Realme 7 Pro release date is 2020 September 01. This smartphone comes with two variants like 6/128GB RAM and 8/128GB RAM. It is available at one color options like black.

  • Diaplay: 6.4 inches
