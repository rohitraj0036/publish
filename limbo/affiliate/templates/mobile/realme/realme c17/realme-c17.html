Here Rumours comes out Realme is about to launch its new smartphone, Realme C17 soon.

About Realme C17

Realme is going to launch Realme C17 soon, taking its C series one step further. Realme C17 smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system (Android 10). The phone is powered by an Octa-core (4x1.8 GHz Kryo 240 & 4x1.6 GHz Kryo 240) processor and it runs on Qualcomm SM8250 Snapdragon 460 (11 nm).

Realme C17 smartphone has an IPS LCD display with 720 x 1600 pixels screen resolution and 402 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 8-megapixel primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 13 megapixel wide-angle, 8 megapixels ultra-wide, 2-megapixels micro, and 2-megapixels depth. camera with features like Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus. It has support for 18W fast charging and non-removable 5000 mAh battery.

Realme C17 price in India.

Realme C17 price in India start from Rs. 12,999 and Realme C17 release date is 2020 September 20. This smartphone comes with single variants like 6/128GB RAM. It is available at two color options like Navy Blue and Lake Green.
