About Xiaomi Mi 11

Here a lot of leaks have come out about Xiaomi Mi 11 its specification amazes you. Xiaomi Mi 11 smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system Android 10, MIUI 12. The phone is powered by an Octa-core (2x2.2 GHz Kryo 570 & 6x1.8 GHz Kryo 570) and it runs on Qualcomm Snapdragon 888.

Xiaomi Mi 11 smartphone has an IPS LCD display with 1080 x 2400 pixels screen resolution and 395 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 16-megapixel wide primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 108 megapixel wide-angle, 8-megapixel ultrawide-angle, 2-megapixel depth, and 2-megapixel micro camera setup of Xiaomi Mi 11 include Dual-LED dual-tone flash, HDR, panorama, and more. It has support for 33W TurboPower fast charging support and non-removable 4820 mAh battery.

Xiaomi Mi 11 price in India.

Xiaomi Mi 11 price in India start from Rs. 17,960 and Xiaomi Mi 11 expected release date is 29 December 2020. This smartphone comes with three variants like 128GB 6GB RAM, 128GB 8GB RAM, and 256GB 8GB RAM. It is available at three color options like Gray, Blue, and Red/Mint.

  • Diaplay: 6.67 inches
  • Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon 750G (7 nm)
  • primary camera: 108MP + 8MP + 2MP + 2MP
  • selfie camera: 16MP
