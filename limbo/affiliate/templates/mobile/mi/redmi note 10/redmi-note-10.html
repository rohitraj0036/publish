About Mi Note 10

Xiaomi Mi Note 10 was launched on 6th nov 2019. Xiaomi Mi Note 10 comes with a 6.47 (in inch) touchscreen display. The phone is powered by a 2.2GHz octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 730G processor. The Xiaomi Mi Note 10 runs Android 10 and is powered by a 5260mAh non-removable battery. The Xiaomi Mi Note 10 has supports for fast charging.

Xiaomi Mi Note 10 smartphone has an AMOLED display. It has a resolution of 1080x2340 pixels and 395 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get an 32-megapixel primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 108 megapixel wide-angle, 20 megapixels ultra-wide, 12-megapixel depth and 5 megapixels micro. camera with features like Digital Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus. It has support for fast charging and has a non-removable 5260 mAh battery.
