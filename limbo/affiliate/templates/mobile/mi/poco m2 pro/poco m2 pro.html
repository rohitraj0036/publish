About POCO M2 Pro

Xiaomi is now going to launch a new smartphone, takes its Poco series one step further, its name is Poco 3. Poco M2 Pro smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system (Android 10). The phone is powered by an Octa-core (2x2.3 GHz Kryo 465 Gold & 6x1.8 GHz Kryo 465 Silver) processor and it runs on Qualcomm SM7125 Snapdragon 720G (8 nm) Chipset.

Poco M2 Pro smartphone has an IPS LCD display with 1080 x 2400 pixels screen resolution and 395 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get an 16-megapixel primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 48 megapixel wide-angle, 8 megapixels ultra-wide, 5-megapixel micro and 2 megapixels depth. camera with features like Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus. It has support for 33W fast charging and has a non-removable 5000 mAh battery.

Poco M2 Pro price in India.

Poco M2 Pro price in India may be Rs 13,999 and Poco M2 Pro release date may be 2020 July 07. This smartphone comes with three variant like 4/64GB RAM, 6/64GB RAM, 6/128GB RAM. It is available at 3 colour options like Two Shades of Black, Out Of The Blue, and Green and Greener.

  • Diaplay: 6.47 inches
