About POCO c3

Xiaomi is now going to launch a new smartphone, takes its Poco series one step further, its name is Poco 3. Xiaomi Poco C3 smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system (Android 10). The phone is powered by an octa-core (2 GHz, Dual core, Cortex A75 + 1.8 GHz, Hexa Core, Cortex A55) processor and it runs on MediaTek Helio G80 Chipset.

Xiaomi POCO C3 smartphone has a IPS LCD display. 1080 x 2340 Pixels and 395 ppi pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 8-megapixel primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 13 megapixel wide-angle, 8 megapixels ultra-wide, 5-megapixel depth and 2 megapixels micro. camera with features like Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus. It has support for fast charging and has a non-removable 5020 mAh battery.

Xiaomi Poco c3 price in India may be Rs 8,499 and its release date may be October 23, 2020 (unofficial). This smartphone comes with 1 variants like 3/32 GB. Xiaomi Poco C3 which is expected to available in Black, Gold, orange colour.

  • Diaplay: 6.53 inches
  • Processor: MediaTek Helio G80
