About Moto G 5G

Here a lot of leaks have come out about Moto G 5G its specification amazes you. Moto G 5G smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system Android 10. The phone is powered by an Octa-core (1x2.4 GHz Kryo 475 Prime & 1x2.2 GHz Kryo 475 Gold & 6x1.8 GHz Kryo 475 Silver) and it runs on Qualcomm Snapdragon 765G (7 nm).

Moto G 5G smartphone has an LTPS display with 1080 x 2400 pixels screen resolution and 409 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 16-megapixel wide primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 48 megapixel wide-angle, 8-megapixel ultrawide-angle, and 2-megapixel micro camera setup of Moto G 5G include HDR, panorama, dual-LED dual-tone flash, and more. It has support for 20W TurboPower fast charging support and non-removable 5000 mAh battery.

Moto G 5G price in India.

Moto G 5G price in India start from Rs. 20,999 and Moto G 5G expected release date is 30 November 2020. This smartphone comes with single variants like 4/64GB RAM and 6/128GB RAM. It is available at two color options like Volcanic Gray, and Frosted Silver.

  • Diaplay: 6.7 inches
  • Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon 765G (7 nm)
  • primary camera: 48MP + 8MP + 2MP

  • Battery: Non-removable Li-Ion 5000 mAh battery
