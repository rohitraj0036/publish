About Micromax In 1b

Here a lot of leaks have come out about Micromax In 1b its specification amazes you. Micromax In 1b smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system (Android 10 Go Edition). The phone is powered by an Octa-core (4x2.3 GHz Cortex-A53 & 4x1.8 GHz Cortex-A53) processor and it runs on MediaTek Helio G35 (MT6765V/WB).

Micromax In 1b smartphone has an IPS LCD display with 720x1600 pixels screen resolution and 269 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 8-megapixel primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 13 megapixel wide-angle and 2-megapixels depth. camera with features like Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus, and Continuos Shooting. It has support for fast charging and non-removable 4100 mAh battery.
