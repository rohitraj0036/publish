About OnePlus 9

Here a lot of leaks have come out about OnePlus 9 its specification amazes you. OnePlus 9 smartphone runs on the latest version of the Android operating system Android v11 (Q). The phone is powered by an Octa-core 3.1GHz Single core Kryo 585, a 2.42GHz Tri core Kryo 585 with a 1.8GHz Quad-core Kryo 585 processor configuration and it runs on Qualcomm Snapdragon 875.

OnePlus 9 smartphone has an AMOLED display with 1080 x 2400 pixels screen resolution and 396 PPI pixel density. Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 16-megapixel primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a 64 megapixel wide-angle, 16-megapixels ultra-wide, 8-megapixel depth and 2-megapixel micro with an UltraPixel BSI image sensor. camera with features like HDR mode, auto flash, exposure compensation, continuous shooting, and more. It has support for fast charging and non-removable 5000 mAh battery.
