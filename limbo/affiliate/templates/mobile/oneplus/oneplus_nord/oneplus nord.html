About OnePlus nord

OnePlus has launched a new smartphone on 21 july 2020 called OnePlus Nord. OnePlus nord price in india 24,999-INR and OnePlus Nord can be released by the end of August. Lets talk about its design: OnePlus Nord will feature a curvy glass back as well as a vertical allied quad-camera setup at its top right corner. It will have a 6.4(inch) Full-HDplus AMOLED touchscreen display with Gorilla Glass 5 protection. The Nopelus Nord is 8.2mm thick and has a weight of 182g, which is suitable for single-handed use.

Expressing about the camera, OnePlus Nord comes with a quad camera setup that includes a 48 MP camera, 8 MP ultra-wide angle lens, 5 MP depth shooter, and a 2 MP macro lens. On the front, the phone will feature a dual punch hole camera set up that will include a 32 MP main sensor and an 8 MP ultra-wide.

This smartphone comes with three variant 6/64GB RAM, 8/128GB RAM, 12/256GB RAM with colour options like Blue-Marble, Gray-Onyx. The smart -phone is powered by the Qualcomm SDM765 Snapdragon 765G and Non-removable Li-Po 4115-mAh battery. It has support for fast charging 30W, 70% complete in 30 min.
