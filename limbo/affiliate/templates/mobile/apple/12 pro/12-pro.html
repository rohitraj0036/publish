About iPhone 12 Pro

Apple is going to launch iPhone 12 Pro soon, taking iPhone 12 series one step further. iPhone 12 Pro smartphone runs on the latest version of the iOS 14.1. The phone runs on Apple A14 Bionic (5 nm) Chipset.

iPhone 12 Pro smartphone has Super Retina XDR OLED touchscreen display that has a resolution of 1170 x 2532 pixels at a pixel density of 460 pixels per inch (ppi). Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 12 MP camera with f/2.2 aperture primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a dual camera with 12 MP primary camera with f/1.6 aperture, 12 MP camera with f/2.4 aperture and a 12 MP camera with f/2.0 aperture. camera with features like Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focusLens correction (Ultra Wide), Advanced red-eye correction, Photo geotagging, Auto image stabilisation, and Burst mode. It has support for 15W fast charging and non-removable 3100 mAh battery.

iPhone 12 Pro price in India.

iPhone 12 Pro price in India start from Rs. 119,900 and iPhone 12 Pro release date is release 2020, October 23 ( Expected Date ). This smartphone comes with three variants like 128GB 4GB RAM, 256GB 4GB RAM, and 512GB 4GB RAM. It is available at four color options like Silver, Graphite, Gold, and Pacific Blue.
