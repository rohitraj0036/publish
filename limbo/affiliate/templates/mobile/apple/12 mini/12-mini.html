About iPhone 12 mini

Apple is going to launch iPhone 12 mini soon, taking iPhone series one step further. iPhone 12 mini smartphone runs on the latest version of the iOS 14.1. The phone runs on Apple A14 Bionic (5 nm) Chipset.

iPhone 12 mini smartphone has Super Retina XDR OLED touchscreen display that has a resolution of 1080 x 2340 pixels at a pixel density of 460 pixels per inch (ppi). Talking about its camera, On the front camera, you get a 12 MP camera with f/2.2 aperture primary camera and on the rear camera, there is a dual camera with 12 MP primary camera with f/1.6 aperture and a 12 MP camera with f/2.4 aperture. camera with features like Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus. It has support for 20W fast charging and non-removable 3100 mAh battery.

iPhone 12 mini price in India.

iPhone 12 mini price in India start from Rs. 69,900 and iPhone 12 mini release date is release 2020, November 6 ( Expected Date ). This smartphone comes with three variants like 64GB 4GB RAM, 128GB 4GB RAM, and 256GB 4GB RAM. It is available at five color options like Black, White, Red, Green, and Blue.
