Apple 11 pro max summary

Apple iPhone 11 Pro max smartphone was launched in 2019, September 10. The phone comes with a 6.5-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1242 x 2688 pixels at a pixel density of 458 pixels per inch (ppi). Apple iPhone SE is powered by an Apple A13 Bionic (7 nm+) processor. It comes with 4GB of RAM.

Apple iPhone 11 pro max iOS 13, upgradable to iOS 13.5 and packs 64GB of inbuilt storage. The Apple iPhone 11 pro max is a single SIM or Dual SIM (GSM) smartphone that accepts a Nano-SIM card. The Apple iPhone 11 pro max measures 1158 x 77.8 x 8.1 mm (height x width x thickness) and weighs 226 grams. It was launched in Space Gray, Silver, Gold, Midnight Green (matte colors).

As far as the cameras are concerned, the Apple iPhone 11 pro max on the rear packs 12-megapixel camera and 12-megapixel selfies camera

Connectivity options on the Apple iPhone 11 pro max include Wi-Fi, GPS, Bluetooth v5.0, NFC, 3G, and 4G. Sensors on the phone include Face ID, accelerometer, ambient light sensor, gyroscope, proximity sensor, and compass/ magnetometer.
