After ban PUBG, A new game is going to be released, named FAU-G.

Written By Rohit Raj- Updated on 6 September 2020

After banning PUBG, a new game FAU-G is about to be released soon, which Akshay Kumar has tweeted on his social media. The game is designed by an Indian developer, an initiative supporting Prime Minister Narendra Modi's Atmanihar movement.

A new game FAU-G stands for Fearless And United-Guards.

Image source | google uploaded by: thesocialdigital

Government of India has banned 118 apps with PUBG. Chinese company Tencent has suffered losses since PUBG's ban. Taking advantage of this after PUBJ's ban, Akshay Kumar announced the release of the FAU-G game on his social media account, which brought immense joy to Indian gamers.

FAU-G Game developed by Bengaluru-headquartered nCORE Games which is an Indian company and 20% of the net revenue generated will be donated to "Bharat Ke Veer Trust" which is a great idea.
